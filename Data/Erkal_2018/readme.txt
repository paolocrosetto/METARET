Erkal, Gangadharan, and Koh (2018, EER): We used a Gneezy and Potters investment task to elicit risk preferences. The endowment is 50 ECU, and the multiplier if the investment succeed is x3. Probability of the investment succeeding is 0.5. The relevant variable is called RiskInvest and RiskKeep, where both variables should add up to 50. Attachments: 1 x PDF paper; 1 x supplementary.zip.
