id: id number of subject.

risk_gain: row in which subject switches from lottery to sure amount in the Risk attitude elicitation (gain domain), 1-10. 10 indicates no switch. Missing values indicate subjects who switch more than once. 

risk_loss: row in which subject switches from lottery to sure amount in the Risk attitude elicitation (loss domain), 1-10. 10 indicates no switch. Missing values indicate subjects who switch more than once.

age: subject age in years.

gender: 1=male, 0=female.
